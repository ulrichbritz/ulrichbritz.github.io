Andromaverse

I spent 2 years working for Andromaverse on an exciting 2.5D open-world RPG game built on the blockchain. It incorporated the innovative use of Crypto transactions and NFTs from the exclusive Andromaverse Collection.

In this immersive gaming experience, players would have embarked on a journey of discovery and exploration, roaming through a captivating open world. They would engage in various activities such as breeding, evolving, battling, and crafting, forming the core gameplay loops.

As the then sole programmer on the game design team, I had the privilege of working alongside a highly talented group of artists, animators, and other individuals. Together, we were dedicated to bringing the Andromaverse to life, creating a rich and engaging gaming universe.

Dice-Fall-Chronicles

A 3rd person Fantasy RPG which includes tactical battles, I was working on in my spare time to learn more about turn based development while playing around with some DnD mechanics.

Here are some of the key features implemented in the game:

- A smooth Camera and Movement system, both in free roam and tactical format.

- An intuitive Inventory and Equipment System that allows players to manage and organize their items and gear efficiently.

- A Branching Dialogue System. Where NPC's are fit with Animation Rigging.

- A turn-based tactical Battle System that encompasses a wide range of actions, including light and heavy attacks, managing stamina, health, and mana, executing powerful abilities.

- An intelligent AI System, where enemies react strategically based on angle and distance, engage in pursuit, incorporate field of view, and employ an alert system for added challenge.

- A Day/Night and date system.
