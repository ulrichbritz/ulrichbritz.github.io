Andromaverse

My current project at Andromaverse Labs is an exciting 2.5D open-world RPG game built on the block-chain. It incorporates the innovative use of Crypto transactions and NFTs from the exclusive Andromaverse Collection.

In this immersive gaming experience, players will embark on a journey of discovery and exploration, roaming through a captivating open world. They will engage in various activities such as breeding, evolving, battling, and crafting, forming the core gameplay loops.

Into the Unknown: A Wild Gathering

In my spare time, I am currently dedicated to developing an impressive 3rd person souls-like RPG, which is nearing completion and will soon be showcased as a remarkable tech demo.


Here are some of the key features already implemented in the game:


A robust and seamless Camera System that enhances the player's perspective and immersion.


A fluid and responsive Movement System, ensuring precise control and smooth navigation.


An intuitive Inventory System that allows players to manage and organize their items efficiently.


A comprehensive Equipment System that enables players to equip and customize their characters with various gear and upgrades.


An intricate Ability System that empowers players with unique skills and techniques.


A dynamic Battle System that encompasses a wide range of actions, including light and heavy attacks, managing stamina, health, and mana, executing powerful abilities, and executing agile maneuvers such as jumping, dodging, and rolling.


An intelligent AI System, where enemies react strategically based on angle and distance, engage in pursuit, utilize sleeping states, incorporate field of view, and employ an alert system for added challenge.
